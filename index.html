How to Play

Start with entering you and your friend's names, choose your theme, pick the difficulty, then play!

Scoring

Add a point when someone gets the question correct! Scoring for each question goes as follows:

Easy: 1 pt

Medium: 3 pts

Hard: 5 pts

Other Ways to Play

Competitive

Only give points to the person who can answer the question first!
